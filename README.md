# Gem Watermark Remover

Free online tool to remove Gemini AI watermarks from images. Fast, secure, and browser-based, no sign-up required.

🔗 Live tool: https://gemwatermark.com

## About

Gemini adds a visible sparkle watermark to every AI-generated image. Gem Watermark Remover helps creators, designers, and marketers get a clean, watermark-free version of their images in seconds, without needing any design software or technical skills.

## Features

- Removes Gemini and Nano Banana AI watermarks
- Supports PNG, JPG, and WEBP formats
- Handles images up to 25MB
- No account or sign-up required
- Files are processed securely and not stored
- Simple drag-and-drop interface
- Available in multiple languages

## How it works

1. Upload your Gemini-generated image
2. The tool detects and removes the watermark
3. Download your clean, watermark-free image

## Why remove the watermark

Many designers, marketers, and content creators need clean images for client presentations, mockups, and social media posts, where a visible AI watermark is not appropriate. Gem Watermark Remover makes this quick and hassle-free.

## Privacy

Images are processed securely and are not stored or shared. See the full privacy policy on the website for details.
